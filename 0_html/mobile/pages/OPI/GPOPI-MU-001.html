GP Open Innovation

Open Innovation Matching Program

For Korean startups and global companies

GP Open Innovation Matching Program is a program that helps promising startups in Korea participate in Open Innovation challenges that global companies worldwide provide to KOTRA.

Through this program, Korean startups are given the chance to cooperate with global companies to prove their technologies in the overseas market and global companies can find the best-fit startups for Open Innovation challenges.

If you are a Korean startup and you want to prove your technologies to global companies, or a global company looking for a startup that fits into Open Innovation challenges, please send us an email using the form below.
